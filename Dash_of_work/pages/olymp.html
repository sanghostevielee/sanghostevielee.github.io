This used to be a train station and now it’s not. What's good about an abandoned station? That it’s abandoned! Apparently the municipality doesn’t care about this place and people just started to have fun here. Enjoy the crooked urban scenery of graffiti, a ruin of cement buildings and thrown away furniture along the railway.
