Billy's Café & Kiosk

“

DASHA The problem of choice.
STEVIE Their season's special menu is a must!

”

Now when I am trying to write this story I can’t say that I remember that day. Only two things: it was cold and we had a Unit test of Principles of Databases. Very nice beginning, isn’t it?

After the test, I decided to join Stevie’s “coffee tour”. He told that the place where we were going was considered the place with the best coffee in Minich. The best coffee in my life I tried in Minsk. Okay, let's see how good Munich's is.

Although it was already winter day the “Fall Specials” were suggested. I could not decide between “pumpkin spice latte” and “white choc cortado”. Actually, I am not really a big fan of pumpkin but I am a fan of “How I met your mother” and the pumpkin latte is Marshall’s favorite beverage. It was the hardest decision of my life :) But agree “white choc cortado” sounds fascinating. Of course, there were “usual” cappuccino, latte, and nice-looking desserts.

By the way, 100 points go to Gryffindor Billy's Café & Kiosk! You know why? You will never freeze waiting for your coffee.

What about coffee itself? It was definitely nice, very creamy with a citrus taste. Actually, because of COVID-19 Billy's Café & Kiosk is the only place where I tried coffee so far, so I can say that they make the best coffee in Munich. If you don’t trust me, go there and check :) Thanks Stevie for bringing me there.

— Dasha
