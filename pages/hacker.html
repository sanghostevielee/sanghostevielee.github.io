Hackerbrücke

“

LIAM There was no sunset
STEVIE Google Maps say it's closed, but it's not!
DASHA How did they climb there?

”

Why didn’t I ever think of actually coming here despite my numerous travels on the S-Bahn? This would be a perfect sunset spot – it’s facing west with an open view and the bridge itself is quite sexy as well.

I wanted to climb the metal structure and sit on it like teenagers would do, but I stopped because I didn’t want to be stopped by the police even before getting my residence permit. So maybe next time.

The best thing about this bridge is that it is laid over across the railway and you can access it directly from S-Bahn station. You just climb the stairs and that’s it. The downside of this is that there’s nothing around other than a nice-looking Biergarten that is surely closed in these times. So maybe this whole post should rather be published when COVID is over?

Nonetheless, I find it pleasant to have some kind of a vision (or a fantasy) about how this city would look like after this suckfest finally ends. I’m dreaming about a fine summer evening, me climbing the iron bars of Hackerbrücke without any worries about having to get home before 9 pm, and with the residence permit glued on my forehead. How about you? :)

— Stevie
